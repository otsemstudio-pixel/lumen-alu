Lumen Team Solution

Our Mission

Our mission is to solve Uganda's youth unemployment crisis, because job creation is not just an economic issue, it is a matter of national survival. A country that cannot employ its youth cannot grow, cannot stabilize, and cannot develop. Through bold research, community-driven advocacy, and innovative solutions, we work to create real jobs, restore dignity, and build the stable future Uganda's young people deserve.

Problem Statement

In Kiruhura, Uganda, thousands of young people wake up every day with energy, ambition, and dreams but nowhere to direct them. There are no factories, no training centers, no offices. Just farms that barely feed families, let alone build futures. Nationally, 16.1% of young Ugandans are unemployed, and a troubling 41% of youth aged 18-30 are not in employment, education, or training programs. More than half of them are young women who face even greater barriers. And of those who do find work, 9 out of 10 are stuck in informal, unstable jobs that offer no security or growth. In Kiruhura, a district largely forgotten by investment and opportunity, this reality is even harsher. Young people are not lazy, They are trapped. Our Think Tank exists to change that, by finding real, community-driven solutions that give Kiruhura’s youth a genuine shot at a dignified future.

Lumen Team Solution

  1. Agricultural Training Mobile centers teaching agro-processing (milk, coffee, fruit) + paid internships with cooperatives.
  2. Financing & Equipment Micro-credit fund + shared equipment rental for young entrepreneurs.
  3. Support for Young Women Childcare during training + mentorship from local women entrepreneurs.
  4. Job Creation Local processing plants to create formal jobs + direct links to buyers.
  5. Job Information Platform SMS/WhatsApp system listing local job and training opportunities.
